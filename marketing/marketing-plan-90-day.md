## 8. CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### Potential Issues

1. **Bot Attack**
   - Response: "Our bot-proofing worked. Here's the data."
   - Action: Share stats showing bot prevention

2. **FUD About Supply**
   - Response: "1M is intentional for scarcity. Here's why."
   - Action: Educational thread about tokenomics

3. **Technical Issues**
   - Response: "We're aware and fixing. ETA: [time]"
   - Action: Transparent updates every 2 hours

4. **Comparison to Old Version**
   - Response: "We learned from our mistakes. Here's what changed."
   - Action: Side-by-side comparison

### Communication Protocol

1. Acknowledge issue within 1 hour
2. Provide initial response within 2 hours
3. Give regular updates every 2-4 hours
4. Post-mortem within 24 hours
